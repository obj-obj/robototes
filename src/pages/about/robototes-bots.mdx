	## 2018 - Griffin
	**DISMANTLED**

	Game: [FIRST Power Up](/about/first#first-power-up)

	Competed At:
	- [Auburn](https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2018waahs)
	- [Glacier Peak](https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2018wasno)
	- [Girls' Gen](https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2018wagg)

	Awards Received:
	- Quarterfinalists @ Auburn
	- Quarterfinalists @ Glacier Peak
	- Winners @ Girls Gen

	Features:
	- 8 wheel tank drive, powered by 6 CIMs
	- Shifting gearbox for high speed and power
	- Pneumatic intake with compliance wheels can pick cubes off the ground
	- Lift system uses two BAG motors and constant force springs to lipointsft up intake
	- Climber uses two 775 pros to lift robot off the ground in 1-2 seconds
</div>
